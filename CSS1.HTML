WHAT is flexbox ?
To align Html elements vertically or horizantally i.e. rows and column and it also helps to give responsivness to web pages.
Why we use flexbox ?
a. To verticslly center the child elements b. We don't need to give width/height it automatically gives equal amount of available width/height. c. Multiple column layout to adopt the same height even if they contain a different amount of content.
